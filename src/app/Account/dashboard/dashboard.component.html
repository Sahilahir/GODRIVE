GO DRIVE




About GO Drive

GO DRIVE is a leading digital platform for local motor driving service in India. GO DRIVE focuses on expert services clustered around home, Life and Self and where the user need is customized. Using technology, the platform seeks to understand the user need in detail and matches it to verified service professionals.

Vision

Create digital platform for booking driving school's appointment for each and every person of the city.

Mission

The mission of GO DRIVE is simple: connect the people to their desire motor school, make them more productive.
